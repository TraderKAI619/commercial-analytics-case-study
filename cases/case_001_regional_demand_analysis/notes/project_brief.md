# Regional Demand Analytics Case Study

## Business Question

How does regional demand vary across different periods, and what might be driving the difference?

---

## Objective

Demonstrate a commercial analytics workflow using public Japanese datasets.

Focus Areas:

- Business Question Framing
- Data Validation
- Hypothesis Testing
- Data Storytelling
- Decision Support

---

## Target Audience

Commercial Analytics Stakeholders

---

## Expected Deliverable

5-slide PowerPoint presentation

Duration: 2 minutes

---

## Key Message

A good analysis should make the next action obvious, not simply explain what happened.
